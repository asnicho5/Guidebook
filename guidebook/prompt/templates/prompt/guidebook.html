Introduction to Prompting
Welcome to the Guidebook on Prompting for Language Learning. This section will include a brief introduction of the guidebook and its purpose as well as an introduction to prompting, LLMs, and ChatGPT. Enjoy!
What is a Large Language Model (LLM)?
A Large Language Model (LLM) is a trained artificial intelligence program that can mimic human conversation and intelligence by using statistical models to analyze vast amounts of data and the connections between each of the data points. A popular example of a Large Language Model is ChatGPT, developed by the company OpenAI.
What is ChatGPT?
ChatGPT (GPT stands for Generative Pre-trained Transformer) is an artificial intelligence chatbot that, when prompted, delivers extensive responses satisfying the conditions of the prompt.
What is Prompting?
Prompting is the act of cultivating, modifying, and then delivering a task or question to an LLM so that the LLM can produce a more desirable response. The same question, when worded differently or when supplemented with extra information, can produce a drastically different output. The more details you supply to the chatbot, the better the response will be given your particular question.
